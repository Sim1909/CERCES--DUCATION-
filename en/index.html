About

The Center for Tutoring and Strengthening Scientific Skills (CERCS) is an institution specialized in academic support for students, especially in scientific subjects: Mathematics and Physics-Chemistry.

Our goal: help young people enjoy science and prepare effectively for official exams.

Our Programs

  • Personalized tutoring in Mathematics and Physics-Chemistry
  • Preparation for official exams (CEPE, BEPC, BAC)
  • Guidance by experienced teachers
  • Support in small groups or one-on-one
